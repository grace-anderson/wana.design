Bodhi Massage | Massage Therapist

Client website

Betty, a professional self-employed massage therapist, wanted to promote her services to current and new clients. She wanted a simple solution that she could maintain herself at low cost. We created the site together on a website builder and, after several casual training sessions, Bhodhi Massage was up and running. Betty now maintains her site independently, with a little encouragement and personal support. Targetted SEO makes sure that Betty's clients can easily find her, and her business is doing well.

About

Hi! I’m Grace, I love coding and creating.

After over 10 years working as a business analyst in large public and private sector organisations, I’ve stepped out on my own to take on remote work including web design and web development projects for selected clients.

Wana is a Māori word (I was born in New Zealand and am part Maori) that means

  • To sprout, come to life (verb)
  • To be exciting, thrilling, inspiring, stimulating, moving, rousing (verb)
  • Excitement, thrill, exhilaration, fervour, verve, gusto, zeal, zest, passion, energy, sparkle, liveliness, pizazz (noun).
